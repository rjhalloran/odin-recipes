Spaghetti Carbonara


Back to Recipes

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's a simple yet delicious meal that can be prepared in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 ounces pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, cook pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and cheese. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta and toss to combine.
  4. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, allowing the residual heat to cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
