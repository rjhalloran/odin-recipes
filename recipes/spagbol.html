Spaghetti Bolognese


Back to Recipes

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish made with a rich meat sauce served over spaghetti. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large skillet, brown ground beef over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese.
